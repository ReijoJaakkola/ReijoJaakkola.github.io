Curriculum Vitae

I will be starting my PhD-studies (mathematics) in Autumn at Tampere University. I will be supervised by Antti Kuusisto and Lauri Hella.

My main research area is the study of computational properties of various logics, such as modal logics and fragments of first-order logic. I am also interested in studying applications of computationally well-behaved logics in fields such as formal verification and database theory.
