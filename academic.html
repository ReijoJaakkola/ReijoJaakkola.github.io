Academic work

My research has mostly been in mathematical logic, theoretical computer science, and explainable machine learning. I have worked on fragments of first-order logic, description complexity, entropy, and logic-based methods for explaining machine learning models.

Last updated: May 9, 2026

What areas do I work in?

Mathematical logic

Formal tools for understanding structure, expressivity, and computation.

Machine learning

What models actually learn, when they generalize, and why.

Explainable AI

Methods for making machine learning models and predictions more understandable.

Complexity theory

What can be computed, how fast, and where the hard cases hide.
